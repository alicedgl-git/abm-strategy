30
Industry
Why: Highest expected value. HR Tech: 67% conversion × $30K ACV = $20K EV. Bottom industries under $1K EV.
20
Current CRM
Why: Proxy for tech stack maturity and PandaDoc fit. Mid-market CRMs (Copper $6.4K EV, Pipedrive $6.2K EV) convert best — enterprise CRMs (Salesforce $0.9K EV) likely already have document tools.
20
Revenue Band
Why: Proxy for deal size and buying capacity. $25M–$100M has highest EV ($4.9K) — matches PandaDoc's mid-market sweet spot.
15
Employee Band
Why: Signals org complexity. 51–200 employees is the sweet spot — big enough to need workflow, small enough for fast deals.
15
Country
Why: Geographic market readiness. US/UK outperform continental Europe — reflects PandaDoc's market maturity by region.
How were these signals chosen? Each firmographic column was tested for expected value (conversion rate × avg ACV). Dimensions with the widest spread get the most points. Behavioral signals are tracked separately to guide messaging, not scoring.

Assigning 150 Accounts to Tiers

Every account scored on 5 dimensions, then assigned a tier and play type

T1 Strategic (Score ≥ 60)

21 accounts
  • 43% S1 rate
  • $236.9K pipeline
  • 1:1 personalized plays
  • Multi-threaded outreach
  • Custom content per account

T2 Cluster (Score 40-59)

77 accounts
  • 27% S1 rate
  • $230.0K pipeline
  • 1:Few cluster plays
  • Industry/CRM grouping
  • Templated personalization

T3 Programmatic (Score < 40)

52 accounts
  • 6% S1 rate
  • $20.6K pipeline
  • 1:Many automated plays
  • Nurture-only cadence
  • Generic content
The math is stark: T1 is 14% of accounts but drives 49% of pipeline at 43% conversion. T3 is 35% of accounts but only 4% of pipeline.

The Buying Committee

6 personas derived from 150 leads — conversion rates and pain points from the data

Champion / User Buyer 25% S1

44 leads | VP of Sales, CRO, Director of Sales

Top pain (from notes): Cost of current tools, slow proposal turnaround, no visibility into deal status

Message: "Cut proposal time from hours to minutes"

Economic Buyer 26% S1

43 leads | CFO, VP of Finance, President

Top pain (from notes): Cost/ROI concerns, CRM integration needs, no visibility into deal status

Message: "One platform replacing 3 tools = faster deals + lower cost"

Legal / Compliance 5% S1

19 leads | General Counsel, Legal Ops, Compliance Officer

Top pain (from notes): Contract errors, version control, audit trail gaps

Message: "SOC 2 certified, full audit trail, clause locking"

Technical Evaluator 23% S1

22 leads | VP of Operations, Operations Manager, Operations Analyst

Top pain (from notes): Slow proposal turnaround, cost of current tools, manual document processes

Message: "Native Salesforce/HubSpot sync, open API, enterprise SSO"

Executive Sponsor 20% S1

10 leads | COO, CMO

Top pain (from notes): Slow proposals, manual processes, cost of current tools

Message: "Digital-first document workflow = competitive moat"

Marketing / Other 25% S1

12 leads | Marketing Manager, Marketing Specialist

Top pain (from notes): Slow proposal turnaround, CRM integration needs, no document visibility

Message: "Branded templates, content library, seamless handoff"

Critical finding from data: Legal has the lowest S1 rate (5%) but the highest avg ACV ($55K) when they do convert. Sales notes repeatedly flag Legal as the #1 blocker. The ABM play must multi-thread Legal from day one, not as an afterthought.

Scale: Paid Social — $11.8K EV

43% S1 × $27.5K ACV. Primary engine. LinkedIn matched audiences for T1, Meta retargeting + lookalikes for T2, Reddit/Quora community targeting, TikTok short-form video.

Invest: Inbound ($6.4K EV) + Webinar ($5.8K EV)

Webinar jumps to #3 by EV — 25% S1 but $23K avg ACV. Industry-specific webinars for T2 clusters. Inbound: optimize /integrations (strongest engagement signal).

Scale: AppExchange — $4.3K EV

40% S1 rate but lower ACV ($10.8K). High-intent Salesforce ecosystem buyers. Optimize listing and reviews.

Focus: Outbound — $1.2K EV (T1 only)

Low EV at scale, but use surgically for T1 multi-threading. HeyReach for LinkedIn sequences. Not a volume play.

Pause: Paid Search — $0 EV

Zero conversions in dataset. Reallocate budget to paid social and webinars.

Deliverable 3

13-Week Quarterly Plan

Three phases: Launch, Accelerate, Convert

Weeks 1-4: Launch & Engage

  • Activate paid social (LinkedIn, Meta, Reddit, Quora, TikTok) for all tiers
  • Launch T1 multi-thread outreach (HeyReach)
  • Deploy T2 cluster email sequences
  • Begin T3 automated nurture
  • Establish baseline engagement metrics
  • First content drops: case studies + ROI guides
Goal: All accounts in motion. T1 accounts aware of PandaDoc.

Weeks 5-9: Accelerate & Optimize

  • Double down on top-performing ad segments
  • Scale paid social for best-converting clusters
  • Engage Legal personas at all T1 accounts
  • Launch industry-specific webinar series
  • Push 50%+ of T1 to demo stage
  • Mid-quarter check: recalibrate messaging based on results
Goal: T1 in pipeline. T2 clusters engaged. Cut underperformers.

Weeks 10-13: Convert & Learn

  • Convert T1 pipeline to revenue
  • Close T2 warm opportunities
  • Document every win/loss with reasons
  • Run /recalibrate with Q1 outcome data
  • Review before/after diff, update strategy for Q2
  • Execute tier mobility for next quarter
Goal: Revenue in. Model improved. Q2 ready to go.
