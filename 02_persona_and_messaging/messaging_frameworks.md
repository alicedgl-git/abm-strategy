# Buyer Committee Messaging Frameworks

One messaging framework per buying committee persona. Each framework defines:
what they care about, PandaDoc's value prop for them, their pain points,
recommended content, channels, and example messaging angles.

*All conversion rates and pain points are derived from the 150-lead dataset.*

## How Messaging Delivery Differs by Tier

The frameworks above define WHAT to say to each persona. The tier determines HOW it gets delivered:

| Dimension | T1 (1:1 Strategic) | T2 (1:Few Cluster) | T3 (1:Many Programmatic) |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------------|------------------------|
| Personalization | Fully custom — references their CRM, sales notes, specific pain | Industry-cluster — same vertical messaging, personalized intro | Persona template — role-specific but not company-specific |
| Who writes it | AE + AI draft reviewed by human | AI-generated with human review of first batch | AI-generated, automated |
| Channels | LinkedIn DM + email + direct mail + exec events | Paid social (LinkedIn, Meta, Reddit, Quora, TikTok) + SDR sequences + webinars | Automated email nurture + programmatic paid social |
| Content | Custom business case, personalized video | Industry case study, ROI calculator | Blog posts, templates, product overview |
| Cadence | 5-7 touches over 3 weeks per persona | 3-touch sequence per persona | Bi-weekly automated emails |
